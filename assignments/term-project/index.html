Featured Temple Gets New Look

by Staff Writer

Did you know that the Oakland, California Temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints— seen currently on our home page—is almost done with an interior overhaul? This writer learned from Sister Carla Allen of the Manteca, California Stake who volunteers weekly at the Oakland Temple that the remodel is almost done, and it is scheduled to be rededicated on June 16, which happens to be her birthday.

"I am excited for the dedication and for the opportunity to return to my work there on Saturday evenings. I have really missed those wonderful temple workers during the remodel over the last 16 months," said Sister Allen.
