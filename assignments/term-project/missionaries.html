Accommodations for Senior Missionaries

We have much to offer any senior missionary or missionary couple for their extended stay. Most locations are withing walking distance of a temple. Parking is available.

The Studio

Designed for the couple who plans to spend every waking moment in service, this economical unit offers:

  • Approximately 500 square feet of living space
  • All furnishings
  • A full bath
  • Central heat and air
  • Mini fridge
  • Dishwasher
  • Stackable washer and dryer

The Single

Our most popular space ammenities include:

  • Approximately 800 square feet of living space
  • Separate living and sleeping areas
  • Furnishings
  • A full bath
  • Central heat and air
  • Full-sized fridge
  • Dishwasher
  • Stackable washer and dryer

The Double

Our most luxurious unit has space for guests from out of town with:

  • Approximately 1100 square feet of living space
  • A large living space
  • A master suite with full bath
  • A second, spacious bedroom
  • Furnishings
  • An additional half-bath
  • Central heat and air
  • Full-sized fridge
  • Dishwasher
  • Side-by-side washer and dryer
