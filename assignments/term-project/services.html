Service is What We Do

We strive to provide our guests with specialized attention. We know your specific needs, and we aim to please. All of our facilities include the following on-site:

  • Access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons. We know that many of you have traveled quite a long distance to be here and might need to freshen up prior to your service.
  • A spacious area for children. If you have little ones that need care while you're here, your caretaker can use this facility. If necessary, we have a list of volunteers in the area who can assist you with that task, short-term.
  • Long-stay units with kitchenettes and laundry facilites for senior missionaries and missionary couples. Check out our "Missionaries" page.
  • A sitting area with a temple theme to receive guests.
  • An eating area where you can serve your large youth group or family members from our catered menu or the preprepard food you choose to bring in. Food can be kept warm in our ovens. We do expect your group to clean up after themselves and please, no extensive prep.
  • A wedding reception hall. Visit our "Reception" page for more information.
  • A family history library staffed with knowledgeable volunteers.
  • Printer access for family ordinance cards

We also offer the following free amenities for overnight guests:

  • Complementary full breakfast
  • A state-of-the-art gym
  • Indoor pool and hottub
  • WiFi
  • Smart TVs
  • Parking
  • Temple Shuttle

We hope to make your stay as pleasant as possible. We want you to visit us again!
