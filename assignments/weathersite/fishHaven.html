The Biggest Fish Story

by I. Tellstory

A resident of Fish Haven just might have the biggest fish story yet.

While on vacation in southern Virginia, Bob Willis caught what he claims to be a 147-pound blue catfish on Lake Kerr. If this is true, he beat the North American record set in 2011 by four pounds. Blue catfish typically weigh between 20 and 40 pounds are usually measure between 25 and 40 inches. If this fish story is to be believed, then it's likely that Mr. Willis' fish is five to seven times the size of a normal blue catfish. Oddly enough, the current record-holder also pulled his prize-winning haul from the same lake during the same month 8 years ago. And what is his name? Will Roberts. This sounds more and more like an unbelievable coincidence.
