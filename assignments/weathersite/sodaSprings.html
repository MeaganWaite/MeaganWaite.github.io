Who Turned Off the Water?

by Staff Writer

If the new mayor of Soda Springs has his way, the town might have to change its name.

Mayor Troublemaker wants to cap the geyser that gave the town its name. He want to rechannel all the energy from the spring to a new mansion he is planning to build for himself on the outskirts of town. The water will flow directly into his personal pool and drain magically into the ground. The heat from the geyser will supply his house with enough energy to power a small country. And he plans on excising a 10% increase in property taxes as well as a a 4% higher sales tax rate to pay for his new accommodations.
