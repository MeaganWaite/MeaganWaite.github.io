Surprise Pop

by Nobody Knows

A man from out of town proposed to his girlfriend of three years last Friday afternoon in the middle of the street on the north end of Preston just outside of town. John Smith said he and his girlfriend, Mary King, were traveling together from their repective homes in Indiana to John's family reunion in Pocatello and stopped in Preston for gas. It had snowed a few days before and John said, "The scene was a perfect backdrop for me. I've had Mary's ring in my pocket for a few weeks now, and it just felt like the right time and place." Mary had no idea this was coming. "It was a real surprise, but I'm so excited! In fact, we may come back to this quaint little town to have our reception."
