Tri-City Area Gets Newest NFL Team

by Dream Big

The NFL has plans to add two new teams to the league, and Southeast Idaho is getting one of them.

The new team will be knows as the Idaho Iguanas. Their colors are pink and purple. To save money, they will not build a new stadium but will practice and play at the three high schools in the area, rotating between them for home games. Peyton Manning has volunteered to donate his time to coach the team, and he has announced open tryout for both the team itself as well as the cheerleading squad. The date has not yet been announced.
